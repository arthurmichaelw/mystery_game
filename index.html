Welcome to Snakes and Ladders!

Snakes and Ladders is a straight forward game.

The object is to get to the final square using a dice roll to move your player forward on the board.

There are few things on the board to help or hinder your progress:

  • Ladders: Ladders help you move up further up the board, helping you to reach the end of the game sooner.
  • Snakes: Snakes hinder your progress up the board, landing on a snakes mouth will send you down the board to the tail of where the snake lands.

Good luck!
